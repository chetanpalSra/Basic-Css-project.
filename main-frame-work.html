Upscale branding, collateral, and website design for interior designer.

Simply Home Decorating is an interior design business based in North Vancouver, Canada. Owner Lori Steeves commissioned Taney Creative to craft a new brand and logo design, custom WordPress website design, business stationery suite, and magazine design.

A sophisticated visual identity was created featuring timeless layouts, upscale typography, and a color palette of soft neutrals with gold foil accents. Shortly after the successful launch of the rebrand, Lori reported that Simply Home Decorating experienced a dramatic rise in new client inquiries resulting in a positive return on her investment.
